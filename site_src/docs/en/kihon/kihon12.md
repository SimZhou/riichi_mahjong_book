# Basic Waits (2)

If you understand irregular multi-sided waits,
the knowledge helps not only when you tenpai with Honitsu or Chinitsu,
but also by giving you a wider variety of attacking options in many situations.

The top two are hard to call good waits.
In many cases, it is more advantageous to lock in iipeikou instead.

and here the answer should be an instant dora discard with no hesitation.

No matter how complicated a multi-sided wait becomes, the basic idea remains:

<strong>do you interpret three identical tiles as a concealed triplet, or as a pair plus one extra tile?</strong>

Almost every complex wait can be handled with that way of thinking.
