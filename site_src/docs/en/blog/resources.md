# Recommended External Resources

This page gathers the books, parlors, magazines, lectures, and online mahjong resources listed in the sidebar of the original site.

## Mahjong Book Guide

<div class="resource-grid" markdown="0">
  <article class="resource-card">
    <div class="resource-card__media">
      <img src="../../images/resource-book-kagaku.jpg" alt="Scientific Mahjong" />
    </div>
    <div class="resource-card__body">
      <h3>Scientific Mahjong</h3>
      <p>A landmark title from the new era of mahjong strategy books. Built on statistical analysis of sixty thousand games from the online mahjong room Tonpu-so, it challenged many older theories with data-driven arguments.</p>
      <p><a class="resource-card__link" href="http://www.amazon.co.jp/exec/obidos/ASIN/4061497650/mahjangcourse-22/ref=nosim/">View details</a></p>
    </div>
  </article>
  <article class="resource-card">
    <div class="resource-card__media">
      <img src="../../images/resource-book-oshiete.jpg" alt="Teach Me! Scientific Mahjong" />
    </div>
    <div class="resource-card__body">
      <h3>Teach Me! Scientific Mahjong</h3>
      <p>A more approachable follow-up based on the classic <em>Scientific Mahjong</em>. It removes harder sections such as programming and formulas, while adding columns and interviews. Its older title was <em>Super Beginner! Scientific Mahjong</em>.</p>
      <p><a class="resource-card__link" href="http://www.amazon.co.jp/exec/obidos/ASIN/4862483720/mahjangcourse-22/ref=nosim/">View details</a></p>
    </div>
  </article>
  <article class="resource-card">
    <div class="resource-card__media">
      <img src="../../images/resource-book-logical.jpg" alt="Introduction to Logical Strategy for Online Mahjong" />
    </div>
    <div class="resource-card__body">
      <h3>Introduction to Logical Strategy for Online Mahjong</h3>
      <p>A mahjong writer in his forties challenged online mahjong and distilled a more logic-based style of play from battling younger players on the internet.</p>
      <p><a class="resource-card__link" href="http://www.amazon.co.jp/exec/obidos/ASIN/4862484840/mahjangcourse-22/ref=nosim/">View details</a></p>
    </div>
  </article>
  <article class="resource-card">
    <div class="resource-card__media">
      <img src="../../images/resource-book-mj4.jpg" alt="MJ4 Complete Strategy Guide" />
    </div>
    <div class="resource-card__body">
      <h3>MJ4 Complete Strategy Guide</h3>
      <p>A guidebook for SEGA's MJ series. It compares actual match data with strong players, includes a contribution by the author of <em>Scientific Mahjong</em>, and also covers three-player mahjong.</p>
      <p><a class="resource-card__link" href="http://www.amazon.co.jp/exec/obidos/ASIN/4894257882/mahjangcourse-22/ref=nosim/">View details</a></p>
    </div>
  </article>
</div>

      <h3>Monthly Online Mahjong</h3>

      <p><a class="resource-card__link" href="http://www2.odn.ne.jp/~cbm15900/">Mahjong Rules and Yaku Explained: Mahjong Course for Beginners</a></p>
