# When You Have Two or More Dora

When your hand already contains two or more dora, it is a chance hand.  
You should aim for the shortest path to winning.

## Do Not Get Greedy

## Build the Hand with Calling in Mind

When you have Dora 2 or Dora 3, even an open hand is still worth plenty, so it is important to play with the attitude that calling is <span class="red">the natural thing to do</span>.

Suppose you are dealt this starting hand in the South round.

The only tile you should cut is <img src="../../hai/pin9.gif" width="24" height="34">.

Lock in the two dora and aim for Tanyao Dora 2, or a 3900 hand by pairing a value tile.  
This is exactly the kind of hand where the value-tile “express ticket” matters a lot.

### Theory Summary

<span class="green">
When your hand contains two or more dora, prioritize speed and winning ease.<br />
You should build the hand on the assumption that you are willing to call.
</span>
